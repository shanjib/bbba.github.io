League Rules

Season 2026 — Official BBBA rulebook

Scoring

  • Scoring is done in 1s and 2s — no free throws.
  • Any basket scored from inside the 3-point line counts as 1 point.
  • Any basket scored from beyond the 3-point line counts as 2 points.

Timing

Each game is 20 minutes of total playing time, split into two 10-minute halves.

  • First 8 minutes of each half: the clock runs continuously regardless of fouls, out of bounds, or stoppages.
  • Last 2 minutes of each half: the clock stops on fouls, out of bounds, and after every made basket.
    • Stopping after a basket is at the timekeeper's discretion — this rule exists specifically for end-of-game situations where a team might try to roll the ball up the court to burn time.

Overtime

If the score is tied at the end of regulation, a sudden death overtime period is played.

  • The first team to reach 7 points wins the game.
  • Standard scoring (1s and 2s) applies in overtime.

Rosters & Substitutions

  • Each team must have a minimum of 5 players present to start a game.
  • Substitutions are allowed during dead balls only.
  • New players may be added to a team during the season subject to league approval.
  • Players may not switch teams or be traded once the season begins.

Fouls

There are no referees — players are trusted to call their own fouls. Respect the call.

  • Only the offensive player may call a foul. Teammates may not call a foul on their behalf.
  • A foul must be called within a reasonable amount of time after the play.
  • All foul calls result in checking the ball up top — no free throws.
  • Foul counts are not tracked.

Out of Bounds

  • If it is not immediately obvious who the ball was off of, the 3 players closest to the ball vote to determine possession.
  • All out of bounds calls result in checking the ball up top.

Check Ball

  • The ball is checked up top to restart play after any foul or out of bounds call.
  • The ball is live as soon as it is checked — there is no requirement for the defense to be in a set position before the check.

Jump Ball

  • Each game begins with a jump ball.
  • The team that loses the opening jump ball starts with possession in the second half.

Schedule & Standings

Each team plays every other team twice across the regular season — 12 games total. Teams can play whenever availability allows; all that's needed is someone to watch the clock.

Teams are seeded 1–4 based on record. Tiebreakers, in order:

  1. Head-to-head record.
  2. Net score differential in head-to-head matchups.

Playoffs

Playoffs are single-elimination — best of 1 game. This applies to all playoff games including the championship.

  • Matchups: 1 seed vs 4 seed & 2 seed vs 3 seed.
  • Higher seed advantage: the higher seed may elect to skip the jump ball and choose whether they want possession to start the first half or the second half.
    • The higher seed may still elect to have a jump ball if they prefer.

🏆 BBBA Championship Prize

At the post-season league dinner — attended by everyone — the winning team's dinner is paid for by the rest of the league. Play hard. Eat free.
